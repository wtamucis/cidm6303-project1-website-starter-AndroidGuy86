Welcome to Grind & Glide

Grind & Glide is a neighborhood space built on two timeless cultures: coffee and skateboarding. We believe great ideas are born over espresso and sharpened on concrete. Our shop blends the energy of a skate spot with the comfort of a café, creating a place where creativity, caffeine, and community meet naturally.

Skate culture has always been about independence, expression, and persistence. Coffee culture is no different. Both reward patience, practice, and appreciation of craft. At Grind & Glide, we roast our beans with intention and curate our skate gear with the same care.

Whether you’re stopping in for a pour-over, tuning a board, or just looking for a place to sit and think, our shop is designed to slow you down just enough to enjoy the moment.
