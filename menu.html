Coffee Menu

Our coffee program focuses on small-batch roasting and ethically sourced beans. We work with trusted importers who support sustainable farming and fair labor practices. Every cup reflects months of work—from farm to roast to barista.

We offer classic espresso drinks alongside rotating single-origin pour-overs. Our baristas are trained to explain flavor profiles without intimidation. Coffee should be inviting, not exclusive.

Whether you prefer bold and smoky or bright and fruity, we aim to help you find something you genuinely enjoy—not just what's trendy.
